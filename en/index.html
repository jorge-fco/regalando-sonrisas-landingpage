How did this project start?

Through donations, we seek to pay for the dental prosthesis treatment that is required for each particular case.

“Success happens in clusters and is born of generosity” Julia Cameron

How does it work?

The process consists of several steps:

1

Selection

We select our candidates through church and families groups, with references that they can’t afford to pay for the treatment.

2

Evaluation

The first appointment will be to take an evaluation of each case and determine the budget for each prosthesis.

3

Donation

Between acquaintances and the website of GIVING SMILES AWAY, one or more donors will be sought to cover 100% of the price.

4

Start of treatment

Once a GIVING SMILES AWAY patient is "adopted" by a "sponsor", that is, 100% of their dental prosthesis treatment is already covered, then an appointment with the Dentist Surgeon is scheduled to start the treatment.

5

Follow-up

Appointments will continue until the prosthesis is 100% finished. At that time GIVING SMILES AWAY disclaims any responsibility for future dental problems that may appear due to this treatment or any other.
